Dal Thadka

A classic Indian lentil dish made with yellow lentils (toor dal), tempered with aromatic spices and ghee. This comforting dish pairs perfectly with rice or roti. Spice Level: Medium

Adjust Serving Size

servings

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak toor dal for 30 minutes
  2. Pressure cook dal with turmeric and salt until soft
  3. Prepare the tempering (thadka) in a separate pan
  4. Add the tempering to the cooked dal
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot
